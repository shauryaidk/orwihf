New Delhi Style Chai

Medium

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 tsp strong black tea leaves
  • 1 inch crushed ginger
  • 2 crushed cardamom pods
  • 2 tsp sugar

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a saucepan with crushed ginger and cardamom.
  2. Add the tea leaves and let it boil for 2 minutes until dark.
  3. Pour in the milk and sugar, and bring to a rolling boil.
  4. Lower the heat and simmer for another 3 minutes.
  5. Strain into a cup and enjoy.
