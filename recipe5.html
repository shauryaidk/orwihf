Classic Cookies

Hard

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C.
  2. Cream together the butter and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually mix in the flour, then fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Spoon dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Let cool before eating!
