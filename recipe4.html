Exam Prep Espresso

Easy

Prep Time: 2 minutes

Cook Time: 3 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp dark roast coffee grounds
  • 2 oz hot water
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Pack the coffee grounds into your espresso machine or Moka pot.
  2. Brew the coffee with hot water until extracted.
  3. Pour into a small cup.
  4. Add sugar if desired and drink immediately for maximum focus.
