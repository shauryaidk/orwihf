Quick Chicken Wrap

Medium

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tortilla or large roti
  • 150g boneless chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 tbsp tikka masala paste
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions and bell peppers
  • Mint chutney or mayo

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken cubes in the tikka paste for 5 minutes.
  2. Pan-fry the chicken in a slightly oiled pan until fully cooked.
  3. Warm the tortilla on the pan.
  4. Spread chutney or mayo on the tortilla, add chicken and veggies.
  5. Wrap tightly and serve.
