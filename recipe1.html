10-Minute Dorm Noodles

Easy

Prep Time: 2 minutes

Cook Time: 8 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pack instant noodles
  • 1.5 cups water
  • 1 tsp butter
  • 1 pinch chili flakes
  • Optional: Chopped onions and tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Boil water in an electric kettle or hot plate.
  2. Add the instant noodle block and the provided tastemaker.
  3. If using veggies, toss them in now.
  4. Simmer for 3 minutes until the water is mostly absorbed.
  5. Stir in butter and chili flakes. Serve hot!
